About

Whiskey Tango Fabrication is a member-run workshop providing access to shared fabrication spaces for woodworking and metalworking projects.

Membership

We offer monthly memberships that provide access to:

  • Shared woodshop
  • Shared metalshop
  • Dedicated space for storing projects, materials, and building workstations

Refund Policy

Membership fees are paid monthly in advance. Refunds are not provided for months that have already started. Members must provide 30 days notice before canceling their membership.

Terms of Service

By becoming a member of Whiskey Tango Fabrication, you agree to the following terms:

  • Membership fees are paid monthly in advance
  • Members are responsible for safe and proper use of all workshop equipment
  • Members must maintain their dedicated workspace in good condition
  • 30 days notice is required for membership cancellation
  • The workshop reserves the right to terminate memberships for safety violations or misconduct

Privacy Policy

Whiskey Tango Fabrication collects and stores member contact information and payment details solely for the purpose of managing memberships and workspace access. We do not share member information with third parties except as required for payment processing. Members may request deletion of their information after membership termination by contacting us at info@whiskeytangofabrication.org.
