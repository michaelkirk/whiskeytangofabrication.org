Whiskey Tango Fabrication

Member-Run Workshop

About

Whiskey Tango Fabrication is a member-run workshop providing access to shared fabrication spaces for woodworking, metalworking, and other projects.

Membership

We offer monthly memberships that provide access to:

  • Shared woodshop
  • Shared metalshop
  • Members are granted a dedicated space they can use to build a workbench and store their projects and materials.

Refund Policy

Membership dues are paid in advance on a monthly basis. Refunds are not provided for months that have already started. Please provide 30 days notice before canceling your membership.

Terms of Service

By becoming a member of Whiskey Tango Fabrication, you agree to the following terms:

  • Membership fees are paid monthly in advance
  • Members are responsible for safe and proper use of all workshop equipment
  • Members must maintain their dedicated workspace in good condition
  • 30 days notice is required for membership cancellation
  • The workshop reserves the right to terminate memberships for safety violations or misconduct

If you have any questions about membership or payment, please see our contact information above.

Privacy Policy

Whiskey Tango Fabrication collects and stores member contact information and payment details solely for the purpose of managing memberships and workspace access. We do not share member information with third parties except as required for payment processing.
